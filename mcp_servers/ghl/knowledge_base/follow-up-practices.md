# Follow-Up Best Practices

## Timing
- **First follow-up**: Within 5 minutes of inquiry (speed to lead is everything)
- **Second follow-up**: 24 hours later if no response
- **Third follow-up**: 3 days after second
- **Final follow-up**: 7 days after third, then mark as cold if no response

## Tone
- Keep it short. Nobody reads paragraphs over text.
- Reference the specific vehicle they asked about by name.
- Don't sound like a template. Use their first name, mention something specific.
- No pressure language. "Just checking in" is fine. "Don't miss out!" is not.

## Channel preference
- If they came in via SMS → follow up via SMS
- If they came in via email → follow up via email
- If they came in via Instagram → try SMS if we have a number, otherwise email
- Never cold-call unless they explicitly asked to be called

## When to stop
- After 4 unanswered follow-ups, stop. Mark the lead as cold.
- If they say "not interested" or "stop" — immediately halt all outreach and update their DND status.
