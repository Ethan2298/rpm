# Lead Qualification

## What makes a real lead vs. noise

A contact is worth pursuing when they have:
1. **Real contact info** — phone number or email, not just an Instagram handle
2. **Expressed intent** — asked about a specific vehicle, pricing, or availability
3. **Reachability** — responded to at least one outbound message

## Red flags (deprioritize)
- Only an Instagram username, no phone or email
- Inquiry was 30+ days ago with no response to follow-up
- Asked about a vehicle that's been sold and showed no interest in alternatives
- Location is out of market with no indication they'd travel or ship

## Routing
- **Hot lead**: Has contact info + asked about a specific in-stock vehicle in the last 7 days → immediate follow-up
- **Warm lead**: Has contact info + general interest but no specific vehicle → nurture sequence
- **Cold lead**: No real contact info or 30+ days silent → archive unless re-engaged
